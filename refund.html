Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

This policy explains how billing, cancellation, and refunds work for Wescove LLC subscriptions. It is part of our Terms of Service.

Try Before You Buy

We offer a free sample of your county's leads before you subscribe, with no card required. We encourage you to use it to evaluate whether Wescove is right for your business.

Billing

Paid plans are billed monthly in advance through Stripe and renew automatically each billing period until you cancel. Founding Member pricing remains at the founding rate for as long as your subscription stays continuously active.

Cancellation

You can cancel at any time — no contract, no phone call. Cancellation stops future billing and takes effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep access to the lists you have already paid for through the end of that period. To cancel, reply to any Wescove email or contact wes@trywescove.com.

Refunds

Subscription fees are non-refundable, including for partial or unused billing periods. When you cancel, you will not be billed again, but we do not provide refunds or credits for the current period or for periods already charged.

Exceptions

If you were charged in error (for example, a duplicate charge or a charge after a confirmed cancellation), contact us within 30 days and we will review and correct genuine billing mistakes.

Questions

Reach us any time at wes@trywescove.com before opening a dispute — we're a real, responsive team and will sort out any issue quickly.

Wescove LLC is not affiliated with any government agency. Business-registration data is sourced from public records.
